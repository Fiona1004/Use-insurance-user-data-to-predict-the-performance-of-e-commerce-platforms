Our platform’s core value proposition is a cross-domain causal sandbox that turns rich, real-world insurance customer data into a credible, e-commerce-style environment for promotion and growth analytics. In many e-commerce settings, the most valuable features for personalization—deep behavioral and demographic signals—are often inaccessible due to privacy constraints and fragmented tracking. Insurance datasets, by contrast, frequently contain high-dimensional, stable “human heterogeneity” signals (risk attitudes, tenure, prior coverage behavior, life-cycle proxies). We leverage these signals as a powerful surrogate for e-commerce “user personas,” enabling rigorous experimentation and model validation even when true retail clickstream data is unavailable.

The platform implements a semi-synthetic simulation architecture: we preserve real covariates from insurance (e.g., age, tenure, prior insurance history, damage indicators, premium levels, and channel/region codes) and overlay a controlled e-commerce business layer—prices, discounts, promotions, units, and revenue—so that ground-truth incremental value (uplift) is known and auditable. This is the key advantage over training on historical couponing logs, which are typically confounded because past promotions were targeted to “easy buyers.” By designing stratified random treatment assignment within operational segments, we create a clean causal testbed for uplift modeling and policy optimization.
